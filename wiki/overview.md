This is a personal knowledge management system for career transition and growth.

To help the user:

2. Build technical skills and confidence
3. Explore SaaS business ideas
4. Establish personal brand through technical blogging
5. Maintain mental health and positive mindset

## How to Use

- **Ingest**: Drop files into `.raw/` and say "ingest [filename]"
- **Query**: Ask any question about your goals, skills, or plans
- **Lint**: Say "lint the wiki" to check for gaps
- **Save**: Say "/save" to save current conversation insights
- **Research**: Say "/autoresearch [topic]" for deep dives
